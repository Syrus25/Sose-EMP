FAQs

1) What is Electro-Mechanical Production?

Ans.EMP is the combination of electrical and mechanical engineering. This program emphasizes knowledge in the field of technology related to the design, maintenance, and operation of electromechanical systems, essentially automation and robotics. These systems incorporate electronic, mechanical, instrumentation and control elements.

3) What is Studied?

Ans.In grades 9 and 10, there are six taster courses being offered by HE-21 schools under two subject areas. Taster courses offer students the opportunity to explore different types of specialisations under two subjects areas: (i) Advanced Technologies: Through ‘Advanced Technologies’, students explore subjects such as Coding, Robotics and Automation, and Electromechanical Production. (ii) Design & Commerce: Through ‘Design & Commerce’, students explore subjects such as Digital Media & Design, Fashion Design & Aesthetics, and Finance & Accounting..
